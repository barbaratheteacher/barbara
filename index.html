About Mrs.Ioannidou

Who is Barbara?

Barbara Ioannidou is a german teacher,who is working for a public school in Thessaloniki.Barbara has worked in her extra time,without getting anything in return than moral satisfaction and has helped more than 400 high school students,over the past 11 years, to get certified in german language.


In a country of depth and deep financial problems,where paid & extra tuition dominates, Barbara has proved to be an exception.


Thanks Mrs.Ioannidou!Keep up the good work!
